Family Heirloom



In the wake of an alien pandemic, your mundane life is torn apart. Infected, helpless, and completely vulnerable, with only the increasingly distant memory of your wife and daughter to keep you going, you set out tasked to create a vaccine and save what's left of mankind. Will your cunning, stealth and intelligence be enough? Or will you have to embrace the illness and the powers it brings to survive?
