About Us


Our team covers many skills with our varied backgrounds, although we have on big thing in common. We all love playing video games. From MOBA games like League of Legends, to simulators such as ARMA III, we all know what has to be in a game to captivate an audience, and excite them.

Our main developers specifically study Game Development, which allows them to set realistic goals, and help our other developers complete vital components for the project. We have people focusing on the graphic design to give us a unique art style, as well as a developer dedicated to the AI of our enemies.
